Pardon the slightly-lame name. Here I have put together a combined 12 tracks of mixed audio goodness for your listening pleasure. I hope you enjoy the mix and please leave a comment to let me know what you think. All the files were at least 160kbps originally, most being at least 192kbps, so it should sound pretty decent. I hope to start making these semi-often if this goes well. However often that means I won’t know until I make the next one ;). Enjoy!
